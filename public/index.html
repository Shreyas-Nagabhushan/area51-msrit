Welcome you all to the Area 51, a platform for students where they can share their ideas freely, learn exciting and resourceful topics and share their skills without any limits!

Presently we have two modes, mask and DM's.

In Mask mode, students can interact with one another without actually revealing their true names i.e with their alias(fake names).

In DM, i.e direct messages, users will have to login or signup with their actual and verified credentials. On doing so they can send or receive messages from a particular user whom they know.
